2026 Social Security Wage Base & FICA Taxes

FICA includes Social Security and Medicare taxes. Key 2026 updates:

  • Social Security Tax: 6.2% up to $184,500 wage base (increased from $176,100)
  • Medicare Tax: 1.45% on all earnings (no cap)
  • Additional Medicare Tax: 0.9% on wages over $200,000 (single) / $250,000 (joint)
