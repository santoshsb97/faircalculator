What Are Business Days?

Business days are the standard days when commercial operations take place:

  • Monday to Friday: Standard work week
  • Weekends Removed: Saturdays and Sundays are not counted
  • Holidays Excluded: Official public holidays are withdrawn

Why Use an Online Tracker?

  • Deliveries promised in "5 business days" arrive accurately

  • Project milestones reflect realistic workday counts

Business Days vs Calendar Days

Understanding the difference is critical for accurate planning:

Type Includes Weekends Includes Holidays Example
Calendar Days ✅ Yes ✅ Yes 10 days = 10 days
Business Days ❌ No ❌ No 10 days = 6-8 workdays

Key takeaway: A 10 calendar day range typically equates to 6-8 business days, depending on weekends and regional holidays.
